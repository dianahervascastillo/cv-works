COMPANY

History

Powerspace was founded in 2008 with the idea that most digital advertising campaigns could be massively optimized through with great copywriting, innovation, and proactive testing and analysis.

Our previous experience in the e-commerce industry showed us that adopting this approach could lead to a significant boost in Return on Investment and an increase in the acquisition volumes at competitive costs.

Powerspace quickly started to offer full-service performance marketing solutions, and managed to run profitable campaigns for its advertisers, initiating long-term acquisition partnerships that still go on today.

In 2013, Powerspace is a profitable and privately-owned successful company of 25 professionals, operating in three markets (France, Spain, and Italy). We are constantly developing our direct marketing and creative expertise, packaging it into full-service advertising products to empower marketers with a powerful turnkey solution, backed by advanced technology.

Like in our early days, we still focus on the core values that first made the difference: long term focus, pro-activity, constant innovation, quality of service and data analysis.
